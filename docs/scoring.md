# Scoring Algorithm

The swim score (0–100) is calculated from six weighted factors specific to Mahon Pool's characteristics.

## Factor Breakdown

### Wave Height (max 25 pts)
Mahon Pool is a tidal rock pool — waves refresh it but too much swell closes the steps or creates sneaker wave risk.

| Wave height | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| ≤ 0.5m      | 22     |
| 0.5–1.0m    | 25     | ← sweet spot: pool refreshes nicely |
| 1.0–1.5m    | 18     |
| 1.5–2.0m    | 10     |
| 2.0–2.5m    | 4      |
| > 2.5m      | 0      | ← access likely dangerous |

### Wind Speed (max 20 pts)

| Wind speed  | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| ≤ 10 km/h   | 20     |
| 10–20 km/h  | 15     |
| 20–30 km/h  | 8      |
| 30–40 km/h  | 3      |
| > 40 km/h   | 0      |

### Time of Day (max 20 pts)
Based on Maroubra Beach orientation (east-facing) and typical swimmer patterns.

| Time         | Points | Reason |
|--------------|--------|--------|
| 06:00–09:00  | 20     | Golden hour, calm water, ideal light |
| 15:00–18:00  | 18     | Arvo vibes, post-work crowd |
| 09:00–11:00  | 14     | Still good |
| 18:00–20:00  | 10     | Sunset swim |
| 05:00–06:00  | 13     | Crack of dawn |
| 11:00–15:00  | 7      | Peak UV, midday heat |
| Other        | 5      | Night swimming not recommended |

### Air Temperature (max 15 pts)

| Temp (°C)   | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| 22–28       | 15     |
| 18–22       | 12     |
| 28–33       | 10     |
| 15–18       | 7      |
| ≥ 33        | 5      | UV risk |
| < 15        | 3      |

### Sea Temperature (max 10 pts)
Sydney sea temp ranges roughly 17–24°C across the year.

| Temp (°C)   | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| 20–24       | 10     |
| 17–20       | 8      |
| 24–27       | 7      |
| 14–17       | 4      |
| < 14        | 2      |

### Cloud Cover (max 10 pts)
Partial cloud is ideal — UV protection without rain.

| Cloud cover | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| 20–60%      | 10     |
| < 20%       | 7      | Full sun, high UV |
| 60–80%      | 6      |
| > 80%       | 4      | Gloomy |

## Tide Modifier
Applied to swim windows (not the main score):
- Tide 0.5–1.4m: +5 pts (ideal pool depth)
- Outside range: -3 pts

## Grade Thresholds

| Score | Grade       |
|-------|-------------|
| 85–100 | EPIC SWIM  |
| 70–84  | GREAT SWIM |
| 55–69  | DECENT SWIM|
| 40–54  | MEH SWIM   |
| 0–39   | STAY HOME  |

## Potential Improvements

- Add UV penalty to score (currently advisory only)
- Add bluebottle penalty (MEDIUM = -5, HIGH = -10)
- Weight wave height higher for experienced vs casual swimmers
- Add a "Lifeguard on duty" bonus (note: Mahon Pool has no lifeguard)
- Incorporate actual tidal data for more accurate window predictions
